WELCOME:Department Of Computer Science


Welcome to the Department of Computer Science located in the University of Agriculture and Enviromental Science Umuagwo, the Premier Institution in Nigeria. The department was established in 2021 and was conceived as a means of training manpower to meet the demand for increasing computerization. The department trains students to create, adapt and manage computation tools that meet basic goals of correctness, efficiency, security, user-friendliness, fault-tolerance and intelligence.


It is my pleasure to extend a warm welcome to all students, faculty, staff, and visitors to our department's webpage. In today's rapidly evolving digital world, computer science plays a key role in driving innovation and technological advancements. We are committed to providing a comprehensive education that prepares our students to become leaders in this exciting field. Our curriculum is designed to offer a solid foundation in computer science principles while also embracing innovative technologies and practices. Our faculty members are distinguished scholars and industry experts with relevant experiences who are dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and community services. They bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom, ensuring that our students receive a high-quality education that is both theoretical and practical. We offer a wide range of courses that cover foundational topics as well as emerging areas such as Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Database Systems, Human Computer Interaction, Web Applications Security, Internet of Things (IoT), Computer Communications and Networks, Digital Forensics and Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery.


Beyond the classroom, our department is actively involved in research that addresses real-world challenges and contributes to the advancement of knowledge in computer science. We encourage our students to participate in research projects, internships, and collaborative initiatives that enhance their learning experience and prepare them for successful careers. Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, or a visitor, we invite you to explore our website and learn more about the exciting opportunities and resources available in our department. We are confident that you will find our programmes, research endeavours, and community to be intellectually stimulating and rewarding. Thank you for your interest in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Agriculture and Enviromental Science. We look forward to supporting you on your academic and professional journey.
